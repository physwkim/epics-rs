The audit targets `db_channel_get_field` (in `src/server/database/channel.rs`) and `dbChannel_get_count` (in `src/server/database/db_access.rs`) do not exist in `epics-base-rs`. The rewrite has no `db_create_read_log`, no `db_field_log` struct, and no filter pre/post chain (`dbChannelRunPreChain`/`PostChain`) — the entire C `dbChannel` filter infrastructure that the lock-ordering bug centered on is absent. Reads in base-rs go through `PvDatabase::get_pv` / `DbChannel::get_*` which acquire `rec.read().await` (the analog of `dbScanLock`) for the duration of the get. There is no separate read-log creation step that could run before the lock. `read_reply`/`read_action` (from C `camessage.c`) have no equivalent in the high-level rewrite, so there is no duplicate filter-setup boilerplate to remove. No fix needed.
