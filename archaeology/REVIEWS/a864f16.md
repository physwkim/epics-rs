The C bug is in `dbCa.c` (`testdbCaWaitForEvent` missing `caLinkInc`/`Dec`,
`dbCaSync` releasing `workListLock` before destroying `templink.lock` and
`wake`). epics-rs does not implement dbCa: a `crates`-wide grep for
`dbCa|CaLink|db_ca|ca_link|testdb_ca_wait_for_event` returns zero hits,
and there is no `crates/epics-ca-rs/src/client/link.rs`. The CA-link
record→channel bridge that this race lives in has not been ported, so
the audit targets do not exist and there is nothing to fix.
