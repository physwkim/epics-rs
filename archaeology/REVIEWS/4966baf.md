# Review: 4966baf

## Verdict
not-applicable — structurally absent in base-rs.

## Analysis
The C bug is the missing `sizv > 0x7fff` cap in `printfRecord`,
`lsiRecord`, and `lsoRecord` `init_record`, where the user-configured
unsigned `SIZV` was assigned to a signed `dbAddr::field_size` (i16)
and silently wrapped negative.

`epics-base-rs` implements none of those record types. A repo-wide
search (case-insensitive) for `printf_record`, `PrintfRecord`,
`lsi_record`, `LsiRecord`, `lso_record`, `LsoRecord`, `SIZV`, `sizv`
in `src/` returns zero hits. The `server/records/` directory
enumerates `ai, ao, asub, asyn, bi, bo, busy, calc, calcout, compress,
dfanout, fanout, histogram, longin, longout, mbbi, mbbo, scalcout,
sel, seq, sseq, stringin, stringout, sub, transform, waveform` — no
long-string variants and no `printf` record.

There is no `dbAddr::field_size` analog either — base-rs uses
`EpicsValue` enums sized by `Vec<u8>` length, not a separate
`field_size` integer subject to signedness wrap.
