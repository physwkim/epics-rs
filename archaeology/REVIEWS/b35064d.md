The `dbEvent` worker-thread subsystem does not exist in base-rs. There is no `db_event.rs`, no `EventUser` struct, no separate event-task with its own exit semaphore. Searches for `db_close_events`, `dbEvent`, `event_user`, `evUser` return zero matches under `crates/epics-base-rs/src`. Monitor delivery is push-based: `RecordInstance::notify_subscribers_*` directly `try_send`s into per-subscriber bounded mpsc channels owned by the subscriber side; subscriber drop closes the channel, no shared `pexitsem`/`stopSync` mutex exists. The "destroy semaphore before signal returns" race therefore has no analog. Nothing to fix.
