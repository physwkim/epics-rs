The `asCaTask` / `asCaStart` / `asCaStop` subsystem does not exist in base-rs. `access_security.rs` is purely declarative parsing (`parse_acf` and `AccessSecurityConfig`/`AccessSecurityGroup` data structures) — there is no separately-scheduled CA monitor worker thread that watches INPA/INPB/INPC inputs of access-security rules, hence no shutdown handshake to deadlock.
