This commit introduces the `dbfl_has_copy(p)` macro to centralize `db_field_log` ownership semantics (val-vs-ref-with-dtor-vs-empty-array) so callers cannot get the predicate wrong. base-rs has no `db_field_log` type (see review for 56f05d7): subscriptions deliver fully-owned `Snapshot` values (`server/snapshot.rs:51`) constructed by cloning record fields under the write lock. The three-state ownership ambiguity the macro resolves does not exist in base-rs because borrowed-pointer field logs are not part of the design — every consumer receives an owned `EpicsValue`. No predicate to centralize, no fix needed.
